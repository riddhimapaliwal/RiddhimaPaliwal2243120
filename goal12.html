SUSTAINABLE DEVELOPMENT GOALS



Goal 12: Responsible Consumption and Production

  • General Description

    • Goal 12 of the SDGs, "Responsible Consumption and Production," aims to ensure sustainable patterns of consumption and production. This goal recognizes that unsustainable consumption and production practices can lead to environmental degradation, resource depletion, and negative social impacts. It emphasizes the need to adopt more sustainable lifestyles and production methods to ensure a healthier planet.

  • Targets

    • Resource Efficiency: Achieve sustainable management and efficient use of natural resources, including through reducing waste generation and promoting recycling.

    • Sustainable Food Systems : Halve per capita global food waste at the retail and consumer levels and reduce food losses along production and supply chains.

    • Sustainable Tourism :Promote sustainable tourism that creates jobs and promotes local culture and products while minimizing negative environmental and social impacts.

    • Hazardous Chemicals and Waste: By 2020, achieve the environmentally sound management of chemicals and all wastes throughout their life cycle, in accordance with agreed international frameworks.

  • Progress


    • Efforts have led to increased awareness about responsible consumption and production, leading to reduced waste and improved resource management. Some industries have adopted more sustainable practices, and consumers are becoming more conscious of their consumption choices.However, the global rate of resource consumption and waste generation remains high, putting pressure on ecosystems and contributing to climate change. Unsustainable consumption patterns, particularly in developed countries, continue to strain natural resources and contribute to pollution.

    • Achieving Goal 12 requires individuals, businesses, and governments to adopt sustainable practices and policies. This involves reducing waste, conserving resources, and making informed choices that promote a more sustainable future for the planet and its inhabitants.
