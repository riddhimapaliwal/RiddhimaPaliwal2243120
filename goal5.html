SUSTAINABLE DEVELOPMENT GOALS



Goal 5: Gender Equality

  • General Description

    • Goal 5 of the SDGs, "Gender Equality," aims to achieve gender equality and empower all women and girls. This goal recognizes that gender equality is not only a fundamental human right but also a necessary foundation for a peaceful, prosperous, and sustainable world. It addresses discrimination, violence, and unequal access to opportunities that many women and girls face.

  • Targets

    • End Gender Discrimination : Ensure that all girls and boys have access to free, quality primary and secondary education, regardless of gender, ethnicity, or socioeconomic background.

    • Education and Empowerment:Ensure equal access to quality education and eliminate gender disparities at all levels of education. Enhance women's participation in leadership and decision-making roles.

    • Health and Reproductive Rights : Ensure women's full and effective participation in political, economic, and public life. Secure universal access to sexual and reproductive health and rights.

    • Economic Empowerment: Enhance economic opportunities for women through equal pay, access to resources, and participation in economic decision-making.

  • Progress


    • Significant progress has been made in promoting gender equality and women's empowerment in various areas, including education, political participation, and economic empowerment. More women are participating in the workforce, and laws addressing gender-based violence have been enacted. However, gender-based discrimination and violence persist, and women continue to face challenges in accessing education, healthcare, and economic opportunities. Cultural norms, unequal power dynamics, and legal barriers often hinder the achievement of gender equality.

    • Achieving Goal 5 requires a multi-faceted approach that addresses societal norms, legal reforms, and structural barriers. Empowering women and girls involves not only ensuring their rights but also changing attitudes and fostering an inclusive and equitable society for all genders.
