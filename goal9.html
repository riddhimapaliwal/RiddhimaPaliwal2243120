SUSTAINABLE DEVELOPMENT GOALS



Goal 9: Industry, Innovation, and Infrastructure

  • General Description

    • Goal 9 of the SDGs, "Industry, Innovation, and Infrastructure," aims to build resilient infrastructure, promote sustainable industrialization, and foster innovation. This goal recognizes that infrastructure development, technological progress, and innovation are crucial drivers of economic growth, job creation, and sustainable development.

  • Targets

    • Quality Infrastructure: Develop quality, reliable, sustainable, and resilient infrastructure, including transportation, energy, and water systems, to support economic development and human well-being.

    • Inclusive and Sustainable Industrialization: Promote inclusive and sustainable industrialization, and significantly raise the industry's share of employment and GDP.

    • Innovation and Research: Enhance research and development capabilities and increase access to information and communication technology to foster innovation.

    • Sustainable Cities: Upgrade infrastructure and retrofit industries to make them sustainable, with increased resource-use efficiency and greater adoption of clean and environmentally sound technologies.

  • Progress


    • Efforts have led to improvements in infrastructure development, innovation, and industrialization in various regions. Investments in transportation, energy, and communication systems have improved connectivity and supported economic growth. However, challenges remain in achieving sustainable industrialization while minimizing negative environmental impacts. Bridging the digital divide and ensuring equal access to technology and innovation are also areas of concern. Additionally, the global transition to sustainable infrastructure requires investment and collaboration.

    • Achieving Goal 9 involves adopting sustainable practices in industry, fostering research and development, and investing in modern and resilient infrastructure. Balancing economic growth with environmental sustainability is key to ensuring that development benefits present and future generations.
